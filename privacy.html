Privacy Policy

Last updated: 12 June 2026

1. Overview

Crono ("the Service"), operated by George Vlada ("the operator"), is a free chronometer that runs entirely in your web browser. It is designed to be privacy-friendly: it does not use tracking, advertising or analytics cookies, it does not create user accounts, and it does not send your timing data to any server. This policy explains what is stored and where.

2. Data stored on your device

To work, Crono saves information locally in your browser's storage (the localStorage mechanism). This data stays on your device and is not transmitted to the operator or to any third party. It includes:

  • the race start time you set;
  • recorded finish times and their order;
  • any participant list you import or enter (e.g. bib number, name, sex, birth year);
  • notes you add to a result;
  • a small record that you accepted these documents (with a version and timestamp).

This storage is strictly functional — it is what makes the app usable — and is not used to profile or track you.

3. No tracking, advertising or analytics

The Service does not load advertising, does not use analytics or tracking cookies, and does not build any profile of you. No third-party marketing scripts are included.

4. Hosting

The Service is published as static files via GitHub Pages. Like any website, when your browser requests the page, the hosting provider (GitHub) may process technical connection data such as your IP address and user-agent in its server logs in order to deliver the site. This processing is carried out by the hosting provider under its own terms; the operator of Crono does not maintain its own server logs of your activity. The application itself loads no external fonts, scripts or trackers.

5. Information about other people

If you import or enter details about participants (such as names or year of birth), that information is stored only on your device, under your control. You are responsible for having a lawful basis to process it and for handling it in accordance with any applicable data-protection laws. The operator never receives this information.

6. Your control over your data

You can delete recorded results at any time with the in-app "Clear results" action, and you can remove all stored data by clearing your browser's site data for this page. Because the data lives only in your browser, the operator cannot access, export or delete it on your behalf. Export to CSV if you want to keep a copy.

7. Children

The Service is a general-purpose timing tool and is not directed at children. It does not knowingly collect personal data from anyone, since no data leaves your device.

8. Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. The "Last updated" date above reflects the latest version. If changes are material, you may be asked to review and accept the updated documents again.

9. Contact

Questions about privacy can be raised through the project's repository at github.com/rungeorge/crono.
